Back From Outreach!

Hello! I just made it back home from the outreach last Wednesday. I had been unable to send another updated during the majority of the outreach because of the nature of the program. However, I am excited to present the video below that gives a look into what it was like.

What Happened?

The outreach was not only a trip, but an experience that grew me in so many ways, taught me to do things I had never done before, shown me the amazing provision of God. We were part of a new movement that is about to take place in the YWAM base in Trinidad. Our team was first in over 2 years that came as a confirmation to what is about to happen at that base. We connected with churches and schools throughout the country to lay the groundwork for what is going to happen with the base. We were also able to distribute bibles to about 1000 children in the schools. There are endless stories I wish I could all tell, but I hope to be able to share them all with you possibly in person while I am back here in the US.
