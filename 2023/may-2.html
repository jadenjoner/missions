About the Outreach

For the first portion of the outreach in Mexico City, we will be staying at a local YWAM base and be assigned to help and serve the base in any way we can, as well as participate in evangelism and teachings in the local churches. I am still not sure what other ministries we will be able to serve in yet, however, I am very excited!

The second part of the outreach will be spent for a month in Trinidad and Tobago where our main ministry is going to be passing out 1000 Bibles to local schools and encouraging the kids to read it. We will also participate in other activities such as teaching, evangelism, and maintaining run-down buildings at the base. The country is a mixing pot of many cultures and consists of two islands, Trinidad and Tobago. It is located at the cost of Venezuela. Both English and Creole are spoken there as it is a former English colony but is very ethnically diverse.
